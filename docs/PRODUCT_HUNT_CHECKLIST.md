# T4 — Product Hunt Launch Checklist

**Project:** Zotopie  
**Role:** Growth Engineer  
**Status:** Operational

---

## Overview

A Product Hunt launch drives a concentrated spike of qualified traffic — founders and early adopters who actively seek tools. For a software comparison site, this audience is ideal: they're building products and need the tools Zotopie compares.

**Target:** Top 5 Product of the Day. Realistic stretch: Top 3.  
**Expected outcome:** 500–2,000 visits on launch day, 50–200 new returning visitors.

---

## Pre-Launch Phase — 4 Weeks Before

### Week –4 (28 days out)

- [ ] Create Product Hunt account for @Zotopie (if not exists)
- [ ] Build profile: avatar (Z icon), bio, website
- [ ] Follow 20 active PH community members in SaaS/tools space
- [ ] Upvote and comment on 5 products/day to establish community presence
- [ ] Identify a Hunter (existing PH user with 500+ followers) to post the product
  - Option A: Ask a founder friend who is active on PH
  - Option B: Post yourself if account has 100+ followers
  - Option C: DM makers of recent related launches to cross-support

### Week –3 (21 days out)

- [ ] Prepare all assets (see Asset Checklist below)
- [ ] Write tagline variants (A/B test candidates — pick 1 on day)
- [ ] Write product description (500–600 words)
- [ ] Prepare 5 screenshots (see Screenshot Guide below)
- [ ] Record optional demo GIF (30–60 seconds)
- [ ] Build "coming soon" PH page to collect upvote notifications

### Week –2 (14 days out)

- [ ] Share "coming soon" page link via X, email, Reddit
- [ ] Draft outreach message for personal network (family, colleagues, community)
- [ ] Prepare first comment for launch day (founder story / why Zotopie exists)
- [ ] Research competing launches scheduled near your date — avoid big SaaS launches
- [ ] Confirm Hunter if using external hunter

### Week –1 (7 days out)

- [ ] Schedule X thread for launch day (6 AM PST / 9 AM EST)
- [ ] Draft email to any existing users/subscribers announcing launch
- [ ] Prepare Reddit posts for launch day (r/SEO, r/Entrepreneur, r/SaaS)
- [ ] Write 3 alternative "maker comment" responses to anticipate common questions
- [ ] Test all Zotopie pages load correctly (no broken builds)
- [ ] Confirm Hunter will post at 12:01 AM PST on launch day

## Asset Checklist

### Tagline (80 characters max)

**Option A (clarity):**
```
Honest software comparisons — no sponsored rankings, no fluff
```

**Option B (value):**
```
Find the best tools for your stack — 119+ expert reviews
```

**Option C (contrast):**
```
The comparison site that doesn't have a "best" tool for sale
```

**Pick rule:** Choose Option A — clearest value prop, addresses the trust gap.

### Product Description (500–600 words)

```
Zotopie is a software discovery and comparison platform with 119+ honest tool reviews 
across 11 categories — from SEO and email marketing to workflow automation and hosting.

**The problem:**
Most "best software" lists are sponsored. The #1 pick paid to be there. The review 
scores are inflated. You can't trust them to make real purchasing decisions.

**What Zotopie does differently:**
— No paid placements. Never.
— Editorial independence: we evaluate tools on actual criteria, not commission rates.
— Real comparisons: side-by-side feature tables, pricing breakdowns, use-case matching.
— 860+ pages covering reviews, comparisons, alternatives, and curated best-of lists.

**What you can do on Zotopie:**
→ Compare any two tools head-to-head (e.g., Zapier vs Make, SEMrush vs Ahrefs)
→ Browse the best tools in each category with honest rankings
→ Find alternatives to a tool you're already using
→ Read in-depth reviews with pros, cons, pricing, and who it's actually for

**Who it's for:**
Founders choosing their first marketing stack. Marketers switching tools after a bad 
experience. Creators building with limited budgets. Anyone who wants a second opinion 
that isn't bought.

**Categories covered:**
SEO · Email & Lead Gen · Social Media · Content AI · Workflow Automation · 
Analytics · Community · Link Tracking · Productivity · Infrastructure · Ecommerce

We're launching today to share Zotopie with the maker community — people who know 
what it feels like to research 8 tools before making a decision. We'd love your 
feedback on which comparisons you'd like to see added.
```

## Upvote Coordination

**Authentic network outreach only** — PH removes fake or coordinated inauthentic upvotes.

**Who to contact (personal, not mass-blast):**

1. Founders you know personally (5–10 people)
2. Twitter/X followers who engage with your content (DM individually)
3. Any newsletter subscribers (if applicable)
4. People who previously gave you product feedback

**Outreach template:**

```
Hey [Name] — launching Zotopie on Product Hunt today. Would mean a lot if you 
checked it out. [PH link]

Genuinely curious what you think of the concept too — any tool comparisons you'd 
find useful?
```

**Never:** Buy upvotes, use upvote groups, ask people to upvote without visiting the page.
